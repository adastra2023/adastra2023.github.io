A site of small discoveries

With the hopes that the pleasant discovery of finding this site reflects the joy I felt in its creation...

Admittedly, this site is incomplete. It will likely always be a work in progress to some extent. In some ways, the thought seems rather poetic to me: this record of my latest projects will be as unending as my constant explorations.
In the spirit of honesty, however, let me divulge the real reason for publishing it in its haphazard state. If I don't do so now, I fear that the temptation to succumb to my procrastinating tendencies will result in my work forever lurking in some hidden folder on my computer.

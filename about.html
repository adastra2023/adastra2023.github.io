Australian-born, of Korean heritage, residing in the United States, I wrestle with the multiplicities of my identity.

I am a quantitative analyst–economics and mathematics are my tools. I am an artist–a conductor and violinist in concert halls and crowded streets. I am a traditionalist, a lover of rituals and customs. I am an innovator, a seeker of new modes of expression for the already-existing.

The turn of the new year has been spent catching up with old friends, trying new recipes (including a foray into sourdough recipes after receiving a mature starter from a friend), committing to my now-established pattern of limiting myself to a "long" and "short" book, and signing up for my first 5k (a brewery run!).

Below is a brief snapshot of the things that occupy my mind nowadays. Perhaps one of them will inspire writing for the Projects tab.
